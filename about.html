About Miango Rest Home

Miango Rest Home is a peaceful getaway located in the serene highlands of Plateau State. We offer luxury accommodation, scenic views, horseback riding, outdoor dining, and unmatched hospitality surrounded by nature.

Our Mission

To provide an exceptional blend of comfort, nature, and hospitality, ensuring every guest experiences serene relaxation and world-class service in a peaceful natural environment.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria’s leading nature-inspired resort destination, recognized for excellence, sustainability, and unforgettable guest experiences.
